Proposition: Climate change is a real, existential threat that is acting faster than we think and may have severe implications for everyone, but particularly coastal communities.

FOR the Proposition

Design Decisions and Rationale:

  • For both temperature and sea level plots we decided to track the change from last year's rise in the respective units, as we felt that would demonstrate a trend in the increase of the rate of global warming over time. As shown in this plot, the overall highest rise is more concentrated in the late 2000s to now compared to before (Score: 2)
  • Both temperature and sea level were initially considered as metrics, but sea level was ultimately chosen because it is a more tangible metric than simply temperature, and has a more distressing potential impact on those in coastal communities (Score: 2)
  • I chose to not make any deceptive choices when transforming the data, in fact, I made the y-axis for the sea level plot start at 0 to be as transparent as possible. Having it start at the lowest value would convey the wrong message. (Score: 2)
  • The highlighted red points show the 5 highest differences from year to year, allowing the reader to track the trend over time (Score: 2)
